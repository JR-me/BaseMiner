Welcome to BaseMiner!
An on-chain idle clicker game. Every click mines real bBALL tokens on Base — one transaction per mine.
🔩
Upgrade Your Tools
Start with a Pickaxe (1 bBALL/click). Mine 100 bBALL to unlock the Drill (3/click), then 400 for the Excavator (9/click).
🏗️
Max Out & Compete
Hit the Excavator and grind to the top of the leaderboard. The most dedicated miners can claim rare NFT badges — including the coveted God tier.
🏆
Earn Badges
Reach bBALL milestones to claim on-chain badge NFTs: Common, Rare, Mythical, Legendary, and the ultimate God badge for true grinders.
